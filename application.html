Working WhileAwaiting a SSN

An individual in Nonimmigrant Student (F-1 orJ-1) status may work while the Social Security Number application is beingprocessed. For more information, employers may wish to refer to the SocialSecurity Administration’s fact sheet titled, “Employer Responsibilities WhenHiring Foreign Workers”. This fact sheet contains information on how to reportwages for an employee who has not yet received a SSN and is available onlineat: https://www.ssa.gov/employer/hiring.html
